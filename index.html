Deck of Cards Workout: Evolved – Privacy Policy

Developer: DDP Fitness Assistance • Contact: ddp_fitness_assistance@outlook.com

Last updated: January 6th, 2026

Overview

Deck of Cards Workout: Evolved is a fitness app. I do not collect, store, or have access to any personal data from you through the app.

The only data collected comes from Google AdMob, which serves the in‑app banner ads. That data is processed by Google under their own policies; I do not see or control it.

Data Collected

Collected by me: None. The app does not create accounts, track usage personally, or store personal information on my servers (I don’t run any).

Collected by Google AdMob: Advertising identifiers and related usage/ad performance data as described in Google’s policies. See Google’s Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

How Data Is Used

AdMob data is used by Google to deliver and measure ads. I do not use or access this data for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

I do not share personal data with third parties. AdMob data is handled directly by Google per their policies.

Retention

I do not retain your personal data. AdMob retains data per Google’s retention policies.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. Ad settings should reflect that it is a general‑audience fitness app.

Your Choices

  • You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (e.g., “Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS, “Opt out of Ads Personalization” on Android).
  • You can review and manage Google ad personalization at https://myadcenter.google.com/.

Security

The app does not transmit personal data to me. AdMob traffic is handled by Google over standard encrypted channels.

Changes

If this policy changes, I will update this page with the new effective date.
